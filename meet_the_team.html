Meet the Team

Balogun Stephen Taiye

A Medical Doctor and a QI Team Leader with a passion to improve the healthcare indices of Nigeria. Currently rounding up his Master degree programme in Public Health from the prestigious “better by far” university. Had his data science training from the trio of Brian Caffo, Roger Peng and Jeff Leek of the John Hopkins Bloomberg school of Public Health, and his front-end programming with Colleen Van Lent of University of Michigan and the W3CX series.

He is both a Fellow and a Member of ISQua, a member of the Society for Quality in Health Care in Nigeria (SQHN), and has the HIFA 2016 Country Representative of the year award.

    • Balogun Kehinde Ayantunde

    A Clinician, Public Health Expert and a Continuous Quality Improvement Specialist with about 7 years experience in both the public and private sectors. He has championed several QI projects in maternal and child health, and in HIV/AIDS prevention and managements.

    He is a certified Six Sigma Green Belt expert, a Fellow of the International Society for Quality Healthcare (ISQua), and also has numerous certificates in healthcare quality and patient safety from Institute for Healthcare Improvement (IHI), WorldBank, RCSI Institute of Leadership amongst others. He is currently rounding up his MPH programme with the Texila American University.
